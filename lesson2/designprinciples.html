White Space and Clean Design

Youtube

The fine use of white space makes it easy to focus on the main message and visuals, and the body copy is easy to read. In general, white space promotes elegance and sophistication, improves legibility and drives focus.

Hick's Law

Lazada

Hick’s Law says that with every additional choice increases the time required to take a decision. The more options a user has on your website, the more difficult it is to use (if it’s used at all). We need to eliminate choices. To make a better web design, focus on eliminating distracting options throughout the design process.
