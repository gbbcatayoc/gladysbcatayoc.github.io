Target Audience

Persona 1: Office Workers
Demographics
  • Male/Female
  • 20-up ages
  • Works full-time
  • Commutes to work
  • Uses a cell phone and computer to access weather daily for commute and other activities
Persona 2: Outdoor Enthusiast Visitor
Demographics
  • Female or Male
  • 25-75 years old
  • Married
  • Extended Family
  • Workers and/or Retired
  • Engage in camping, hiking, fishing, boating type activites
  • Uses a cell phone and/or tablet to access weather daily to plan outdoor activities
Scenario 1: Preston, ID

Residents and visitors to Preston, “Where the city meets the mountains” often visit the Cub River Canyon recreation area and would need the weather for their camping, hiking, fishing, and hunting activities.
Scenario 2: Soda Springs, ID

Farmers and ranchers in the area need weather data to manage their crops and livestock. Drivers in the area need to be able to anticipate road conditions based on the weather.
Scenario 3: Fish Haven, ID

Fish Haven, Idaho sits at the edge of beautiful Bear Lake. Bear Lake is a popular fishing spot. Visitors need weather data to make informed camping, ATV riding, fishing, and boating activities on Bear Lake.

Scenario 4: Springfield, ID

Springs bills itself as “The Outdoors at Your Front Door.” Hunters, fishermen, hikers, campers, and other outdoor enthusiasts need the Southeastern Idaho accurate local weather forecast as they visit the Caribou National Forest and local “soda” springs and geysers.
